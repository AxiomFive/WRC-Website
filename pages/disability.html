Disability Resources

If you need accommodations for housing or academics, you can reach out to the Disability Resource Center. Their email (preferred) is adarice@rice.edu and their phone number is 713-348-5841. You can read some frequently asked questions here.

Examples of academic accommodations:

  • Extended time
  • Quiet room for a test
  • Increased font size
  • Screen readers
  • Alternative submission format

Examples of housing accommodations:

  • No roommate
  • Private bathroom access
  • First floor room
  • Handicap accessible room
