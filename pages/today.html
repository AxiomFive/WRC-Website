Today
Today, Will Rice maintains a rich tradition of welcoming all students to our friendly family. While we still strive for excellence, we continue to evolve and make new memories. Students from over 15 countries call Will Rice home and make our community vibrant and diverse. Having just celebrated our 60th anniversary, we look forward to many more decades filled with myth, power, and value.
