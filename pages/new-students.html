President's Welcome: Juliette Richert

Hello New Phoenixes!

I’m your Will Rice President, Juliette. Congratulations on being placed into the best college! You’ll soon find out that every residential college is fantastic, but trust me when I say that Will Rice, college of Gods and Goddesses, is the best. I, along with every other Will Ricer, cannot wait to welcome you to your new home!

As the second oldest college, we are proud of our passion and our culture; we are a college of Myth, Power, and Value (ask Doc C, our RA Emeritus about it). If you’ve been looking up Will Rice on the internet, you may have read about how we dominate Beer Bike. Our obsession with sweeping (a term you’ll soon become very familiar with) may make it seem like our biggest value is competitiveness. You’d be partially right, Will Ricers love to win, but what we really care about is supporting each other and being a part of a team. Whether that team is your Physics 101 study group, Powderpuff (Women’s flag football), or the Will Rice Bike Team, you’ll always have a network at Will Rice to cheer you on, help you do your best, and of course pick you up when you fall.

My job as President is to represent you at the university level, run Will Rice’s college government (Diet), and listen to people’s thoughts and ideas. Most importantly, I’m a resource for you – please call, text, email, Facebook message, or knock on my door (Old Dorm 110) if you ever need anything! As O-Week gets closer, I’m sure you’ll start to have more questions and experience a little confusion (I know I did). Don’t hesitate to reach out to me!

Your incredible O-Week Coordinators have poured their hearts out into the planning of PyrO-Week. They have crafted a very intentional week to give you a greater understanding of the support systems that exist for you at Will Rice and throughout Rice. The intention of this week is to help you feel comfortable being yourself or reinventing yourself. You’re now part of a diverse community who will challenge your previous experiences and be your biggest cheerleaders as you navigate life at Rice. As you flip through this book, maybe the flame of excitement has already been sparked!

One of the things I love most about Will Rice is how much we support one another. One of Will Rice’s trademarks on campus is being proud of our college - sometimes obnoxiously so. We’re not just proud of the awesome community we’ve built, but we’re also proud and protective of each and every individual. When you get to Rice, you’ll most likely make friends outside of Will Rice, too. Just remember that no matter how much time you do or don’t spend sitting in the commons, you’re now and will always be a valued individual in this community. We’re all so happy to have you here. Welcome to the phoenix family!!

Facilities

The first thing to know about Will Rice is that there are three separate buildings: The Commons, Old Dorm, and New Dorm. If the last two digits of your room number are 29 or less, you will be living in Old Dorm! If the last two digits of your room number are 30 or greater, you will be living in New Dorm!
