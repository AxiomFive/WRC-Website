President's Welcome: Rahul Popat


Hello New Phoenixes!

Welcome to Will Rice! My name is Rahul and I am the President of Will Rice College. Congratulations on being assigned to the best college at Rice! On behalf of every single Will Ricer, we are so excited for you to join our college.

My job is to lead our student government also known as the Diet and represent Will Rice at a campus-wide level. At Will Rice our student government oversees maintaining our facilities, managing our budget for events and entertainment, and discussing issues that impact all Rice students.

While you are at Rice, you will find an amazing community and family within Will Rice. As the second oldest college, we are extremely proud of our rich culture and the strong traditions that we have built over the years. While our competitive spirit might make us the most obnoxious college, it brings us together through IM sports and Beer Bike. However, our community is the crown jewel of Will Rice. Whether it is studying for your next Gen Chem exam or performing for the first time, your Will Rice community will support you along the way.

The O-Week Coordinators have done so much amazing work to provide the best introduction to Rice possible. They have adapted to the different challenges placed by COVID-19 and continue to form innovative ideas on how to make your O-Week the best it can be. I know it can be daunting to come to college where you might not know anyone; however, the Coordinators have crafted many exciting events to help you adjust to and succeed at Rice.

During your time here, I encourage you to take advantage of all the opportunities that you will be presented with both in Will Rice and across campus. This is your time to explore and learn what you are passionate about. Come to Rice with an open mind and you may be surprised by some of the things that excite you. Along with the A-Team, your Advisors, and numerous others, I am here as a resource for you. If you have any ideas for events, things that can make Will Rice better, questions, or just want to meme, please do not hesitate to reach out to me through phone, text, email, social media, or knocking on my door.

While this year may not have gone as anticipated, I am confident that you will be able to turn it around and keep a positive mindset. I know you are all going to bring amazing ideas and unique perspectives to our college and am excited to meet all of you in the fall.

Welcome to the Phoenix Family!

Rahul Popat

Facilities

The first thing to know about Will Rice is that there are three separate buildings: The Commons, Old Dorm, and New Dorm. If the last two digits of your room number are 29 or less, you will be living in Old Dorm! If the last two digits of your room number are 30 or greater, you will be living in New Dorm!
