President's Welcome: Michael Devine

Hello and welcome, young Phoenix’s, to the the College of Gods and Goddesses: WILL RICE COLLEGE! My name is Michael Devine and as President of Will Rice, I would like to be the first to congratulate you on your placement into Will Rice College. But wait, what’s that, you’re saying you didn’t choose to be in Will Rice College? Oh, that’s what you may think, but I am here to tell you that it was your DESTINY to come to Will Rice, because you, my friend, are destined for greatness! You have embarked on an exciting journey coming to Rice University, where you will explore academic heights, meet some amazing people, and challenge yourself to new limits. And I assure you, Will Rice has your back every step of the way!

As the newest members of Will Rice, you are bound to be a little confused about what this whole Will Rice College thing is about. To put it into words: Will Rice College is a tight-knit community, with a strong sense of tradition, and a long history of being the best College at Rice University. Since Will Rice was first established as the second residential college in 1957, it has always been known to have a certain Myth, Power and Value to it. No other residential college at Rice University matches Will Rice in stature, excellence, or Beer Bike Sweeps; which is why you will find that members of other colleges might sometimes be a little jealous of you.

My job this upcoming year is to serve as a representative for the members of Will Rice College to the greater university. I preside over the College’s student-led government, or Diet, and communicate on your behalf to faculty, staff, and organizations within the university. However, I need your help. I want to hear your voices, thoughts, and ideas because whether you know it or not, you will be active members in shaping Will Rice’s culture and community. So immerse yourself in the amazing environment here at Rice University, get involved, and discover your own path. In doing so, you will find reasons to love this place as much as I have come to love it. It truly is an extraordinary community for ALL, and I hope to continue fostering an environment at Will Rice that is inclusive and allows everyone’s voice to be heard.

O-Week at Will Rice College may be months away when you are reading this, but for all of us at Will Rice it seems right around the corner. Everyone at Will Rice is looking forward to meeting you. My hope is that after O-Week is finished you will feel proud and ready to call yourself a Phoenix of Will Rice. In the meantime,while the O-Week Coordinators are busy working to make your orientation week at Rice perfect, just sit back, read through this book, and enjoy the rest of your summer. The real fun is about to begin.

Will Rice Will Sweep!
Michael Devine , WRC ‘18

Facilities

The first thing to know about Will Rice is that there are three separate buildings: The Commons, Old Dorm, and New Dorm. If the last two digits of your room number are 29 or less, you will be living in Old Dorm! If the last two digits of your room number are 30 or greater, you will be living in New Dorm!
